Origami Designs

Today we will learn how to make a simple ORIGAMI car!


Here are the steps!


STEP 1:-

  • Start with a square sheet of origami paper, color side down. If you only have regular rectangle paper, follow instructions on how to make origami paper.

    • STEP 2:-

  • Fold the paper in half, top to bottom, then unfold.


    • STEP 3:-

  • Now fold the paper in half again, left to right, and unfold again.

    • STEP 4:-

  • Fold the bottom edge to the center crease.

    • STEP 5:-

  • Diagonally fold down the left and right side of the bottom flap. This forms the wheels.

    • STEP 6:-

  • Fold down the top part at the horizontal crease, covering the wheels entirely.

    • STEP 7:-

  • Now fold about 3/4 of it back up.

    • STEP 8:-

  • Fold down the top right corner like the picture.

    • STEP 9:-

  • Turn the figure over and now you know how to make an origami car! Just draw on the windows and doors and you can design your own dream car!
